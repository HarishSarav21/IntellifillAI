Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 14.06.2024

Intellifill AI values your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Personal Information:

What We Collect: The extension collects personal information such as your first name, last name, date of birth, age, address, gender, college, workplace, interests, and description.

Purpose: This data is collected to provide a personalized and enhanced user experience.

Settings Information:

What We Collect: The extension collects settings information such as your API key and GPT version.

Purpose: This data is collected to configure and optimize the extension's functionalities according to your preferences.

Input Data:

What We Collect: The extension collects data that you enter into text fields, inputs, and contenteditable elements on web pages.

Purpose: This data is collected solely for the purpose of providing the Save Input, Autofill, and Custom Fill functionalities.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used in the following ways:

  • Save Input: To store the text you have entered for later retrieval.
  • Autofill: To automatically fill in saved information based on the context of the input field.
  • Custom Fill: To insert predefined or templated text based on the field's requirements.
  • Personalization: To provide a personalized experience based on the personal information you provide.
  • Settings Configuration: To configure the extension based on the settings information you provide.

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: All collected input data and personal information are stored locally on your device using Chrome storage and are not transmitted to our servers or any third parties. We cannot see or access any of the data stored in your local database.

Security Measures: We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Third-Party Services

Our extension does not share your information with any third-party services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
